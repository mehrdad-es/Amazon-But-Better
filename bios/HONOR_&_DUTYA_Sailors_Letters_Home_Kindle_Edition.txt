HONOR & DUTY...A Sailor's Letters Home Kindle Edition|by Raine Parsons (Author) Format: Kindle Edition|This is a heartwarming account of the Naval service of Charles Francis McCarthy. He enlisted in the Navy during World War One, away from home and family for the first time. His frequent letters home give a moving account of his daily life while serving on the U.S.S. Bridgeport in the United States Navy. A nostalgic look at life a hundred years ago.
