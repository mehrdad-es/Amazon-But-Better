The Light Kindle Edition|by Raman Vaidya (Author) Format: Kindle Edition|The Light relates the gripping tale of struggle for survival of a young, rural Indian boy, against all odds, who ultimately succeeds in making his own destiny.Vishnu was the last child born to the parents who already had four sons. After the mounting failures of the four boys before him, his mother put all her hopes on him. Poverty and diseases were prevalent at the time. Vishnu lost his parents early in life to the common diseases. He was thrown into a future without any hope. However, despite all the difficulties, he worked hard and became a scientist with a doctoral degree.But, could he fulfill the quest of his mother? Did he live up to her expectations?Find answers to these questions!
