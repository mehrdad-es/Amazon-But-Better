The same as I am going to do tomorrow.When 1959 rolled around the #4 highway connected the Long Beach area to the outside world and, as Bob Dylan would sing… “The Times They Are Changing.”During the 60’s the tourist was invented and all hell broke loose.The rest is history, and man the whole day passed.
